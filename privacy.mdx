## Data Ownership

Your Voqal Assistant is personal and private. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your
personal information is secure. We do not collect any personal information from you unless you provide it to us.
That means your voice interactions, source code, and any other data you provide to the Voqal Assistant are only
transmitted to the [AI providers](/integrations) you choose and are never shared with us or any other third party.
