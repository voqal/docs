To use Voqal, you will need two keys:
 -  A [Picovoice](https://picovoice.ai) access key  (for on-device speech processing)
 - and a [OpenAI](https://openai.com) API key (for transcribing and TTS)

Picovoice offers a free plan which you can use with Voqal.
OpenAI offers $5 in free credit to new signups.
Afterwards you will need to pay OpenAI directly for any charges incurred while using Voqal.

## Acquire Picovoice AccessKey

- Go to [Picovoice](https://picovoice.ai)
- Sign up for a free account or login to your existing account
- Go to [Picovoice console](https://console.picovoice.ai)
- Copy AccessKey

## Acquire OpenAI API key

- Go to [OpenAI](https://openai.com)
- Sign up for a free account or login to your existing account
- Go to [API keys](https://platform.openai.com/api-keys)
- Click `Create new secret key`
- Copy secret key

## Install plugin

1. Navigate to your JetBrains IDE.
1. Press ++ctrl+alt+s++ to open the `Settings` dialog and then go to `Plugins`.
1. Open the `Marketplace` tab.
1. Search for `Voqal`.
1. Press `Install`.
1. Click `OK` in the `Settings` dialog.

## Configure plugin

<img src="/images/configuration.png"/>

Voqal can be configured in the IDE settings under:
 - Settings -> Tools -> Voqal

## Speak up

That's all there is to it. Say Voqal (pronounced vocal) and say hello to your new forever vocal programming assistant.
Voqal is designed to be fully customizable so be sure to let Voqal know how you prefer it to respond to you.
